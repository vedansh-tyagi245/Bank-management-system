Frequently Asked Questions

What is a loan?
A loan is a sum of money borrowed for a specific period, typically with an interest rate, that is expected to be paid back in installments.
How do I apply for a loan?
You can apply for a loan through our online application form or by visiting one of our branches. Please have your identification and financial information ready.
What is the interest rate on loans?
Our interest rates vary based on the type of loan and your credit profile. Please check our loan products page for specific rates.
What documents do I need to provide?
You will typically need to provide identification, proof of income, and any other documents related to your financial status. Specific requirements may vary.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Yes, you can pay off your loan early without penalty in most cases. Please check your loan agreement for specific terms.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or through the contact form on our website. We are here to help!
